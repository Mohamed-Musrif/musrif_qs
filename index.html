About Me

Quantity Surveyor | Civil & MEP BOQ | 4+ Years Gulf & International (UAE/Gulf)

I am a Quantity Surveyor with 4+ years of experience in pre-contract and post-contract QS activities across civil and MEP works — including 2+ years of Gulf experience in Qatar within a main contractor environment.

My strongest area is BOQ preparation — I have prepared full Bills of Quantities from scratch covering civil works (concrete, reinforcement, formwork, masonry, finishes and external works) and MEP items (HVAC, plumbing & drainage, electrical containment and fire fighting). I have also prepared fit-out BOQ sections covering finishes, partitions and ceilings.

On the post-contract side, I have prepared Interim Payment Applications (IPA) — reviewed by a senior QS before submission — and carried out progress measurement for civil and MEP works on site. I have measured and documented variation orders under supervision, and maintained cost records and contract documentation throughout project lifecycles.

I am confident using PlanSwift for digital take-offs, AutoCAD for drawing measurement, Advanced Excel for cost tracking and IPA preparation, and Revit/BIM for quantity extraction. I have also used Bluebeam Revu for PDF mark-ups and have Primavera P6 planning awareness from study.

I hold a Higher National Diploma (HND) in Quantity Surveying — UK-recognised under NQUAL (IPHS Campus, 2025) — and am currently pursuing a BSc (Hons) Quantity Surveying (Top-Up) at De Montfort University, expected completion 2027.

Currently based in Sri Lanka | Open to UAE & Gulf opportunities | Available for online interviews and immediate relocation.
